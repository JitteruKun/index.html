Medicare

Medicare is a health monitoring system powered by an ESP32-S2 and composed of advanced sensors — the MAX30102 for heart rate and blood oxygen monitoring, and the MLX90614 for contactless body temperature measurement.

All sensor data is transmitted to a web dashboard powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash, which provides AI-generated health overviews, analysis of readings, and possible health precautions — making it useful not just for patients but also for remote doctor monitoring.
